## What the tool does and does not do
This tool helps me **locate and verify links to data** held by third parties. It does **not** host, mirror or redistribute any scientific data. When it reports that a resource is available, it means that, at the moment of checking, a link resolved and the response passed a basic content check (for example, non-zero miniSEED bytes or a non-empty station list). This is **not** a judgement that the data are correct, complete, calibrated, or scientifically fit for any particular purpose. You must still inspect the data and decide whether they suit your work.

## AI-assisted development
This tool was developed with AI assistance and uses large language models for query expansion, routing and forensic reasoning. Model outputs can be incomplete or incorrect. The deterministic verification step exists precisely to catch fabricated or stale links, but it cannot catch everything — **verify results before depending on them.**

## Human contacts
Where the dark-data workflow identifies a data steward, it surfaces only contact pathways already published in authoritative public sources, and labels what is verified versus inferred. Do not use this tool to assemble personal data or to send unsolicited bulk contact. Approach stewards through their official channels.
